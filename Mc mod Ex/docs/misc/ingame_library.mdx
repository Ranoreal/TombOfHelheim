
## What is the Library?

<ModAsset width="100%" location="mmorpg:library" />

The Ingame-Library is where all the things from Mine and Slash are listed!
Unique items, runewords, spells etc, you can search any of them and filter to find what you're curious about!

## How to Access:

1) Press [H] to open the Main Hub

2) On the left, click the "Library" Button
